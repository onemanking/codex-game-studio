## Integration Notes

- Studio workflow skills and agents are maintained directly in this repository.
- Browser-library skills are regenerated by `scripts/sync-upstream-skills.ps1`.
- `scripts/validate-skills.ps1` checks skill metadata, relative skill links, agent
  TOML shape, and inventory consistency.
